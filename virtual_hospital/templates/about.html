About Us

At Virtual Hospital, We Care

Virtual Hospital aims to provide a convenient online platform for doctors and patients to conduct clinical consultations online.

Through this, face-to-face healthcare expenditure is controlled, the risk of others getting into close contact with possible carriers of the virus is reduced, and patients can enjoy the convenience of collecting their prescriptions at designated collection areas at hospitals and clinics without having to queue for a clinical consultation.
